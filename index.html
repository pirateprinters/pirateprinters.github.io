Step 1: Navigate to pirate.black via your web browser of choice.

Step 2: Click on "Get Wallets" in the middle of the screen.

Step 3: In this walkthrough we focus on SevenSeas for MacOS so click the MacOS button under SevenSeas.

Step 4: This will start the download which you can see in your application bar.

Step 5: Click the SevenSeas.dmg which will open the installation window.

Step 6: Drag the SevenSeas Icon into the Application Folder.

Step 7: Now Click the Application Folder to view your installed Applications.

Step 8: Scroll through your Applications until you find the SevenSeas Icon.

Step 9: Click the SevenSeas Icon to open.

Step 10: You may receive a security message stating the application can't open due to a non-verified developer, if so click cancel and continue to Step 11, if not continue to Step 15.

Step 11(Optional): Click System Preferences in your Application Bar.

Step 12(Optional): Click the Security & Privacy icon. Click Cancel on the “SevenSeas cannot be opened” message.

Step 13(Optional): Click the "Open Anyway" at the bottom of the Security & Privacy Window.

Step 14(Optional): Click "Open" in the pop-up window.

Step 15: SevenSeas will now open and begin downloading Sprout and Sapling parameters and keys.

Step 16: Once these downloads are complete SevenSeas will begin syncing with the blockchain.

Note: There is a way to speed along this syncing process by downloading the bootstrap files which I will cover in another walkthrough.
